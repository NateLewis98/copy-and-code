Hero Section: A punchy headline and one-liner. Something like "Websites that sound as good as they look" or "Copy + Code for small businesses that want to grow." Then a CTA button — "See my work" or "Let's talk."

What I do — Two or three short blurbs covering your services. Website builds, conversion-focused copywriting, landing pages. Keep each one to two sentences max, focused on the result the client gets.

Portfolio — Shane's site front and center with a screenshot and a brief description of what you delivered and why it works. As you build more projects, add them here.

About — Now you introduce yourself. A few sentences: your name, what you bring to the table, why you're different (the copy + code angle). This is where the photo goes. Keep it warm and confident, not resume-y.

Testimonial — Ask Shane for a quick quote about working with you. Even one testimonial beats zero.

Contact/CTA — A simple form or just your email. End with a clear call to action.
